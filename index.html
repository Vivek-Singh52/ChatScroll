How to Export Your WhatsApp Chat

1

Open the chat in WhatsApp

Go to the individual or group chat you want to export on your phone.

2

Tap the menu → More → Export Chat

On Android: tap ⋮ → More → Export Chat. On iPhone: tap the contact name → Export Chat.

3

Choose "Attach Media" or "Without Media"

Select "Attach Media" to include photos (exports a .zip), or "Without Media" for just text.

4

Upload the exported file

Select the exported .txt or .zip file above to visualize instantly.
